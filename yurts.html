The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt? Our luxury yurts are based on the traditional design of central Asian nomads.
Each yurt is a circular wood-framed tent covered with durable canvas.

  • Our yurts are approximately 16 feet in diameter.
  • Each yurt is built on a raised wooden deck.
  • Each yurt is fully carpeted and has a queen-sized bed.
  • Each yurt includes a small heater and electricity.
