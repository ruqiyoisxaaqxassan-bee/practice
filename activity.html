Activities at Pacific Trails

There is always something to do at Pacific Trails Resort!

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has five miles of hiking trails that connect with the California State Parks.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for rent at the resort office.

Bird Watching

Many species of birds inhabit our resort area, including eagles and hawks.
